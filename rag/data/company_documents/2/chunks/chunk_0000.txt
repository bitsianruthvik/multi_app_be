DISEASE BACKGROUND: CHOLANGIOCARCINOMA & FGFR BIOLOGY Cholangiocarcinoma Overview • Rare, aggressive cancer originating in the bile ducts • Incidence: increasing globally due to improved diagnosis • Median overall survival after first-line therapy: 11–14 months • FGFR2 fusions occur in 10–16% of intrahepatic cases What FGFR2 Fusions Do • Create ligand-independent activation • Trigger MAPK, PI3K–AKT, and STAT pathways • Drive uncontrolled tumor proliferation • Associated with unique biology—more indolent but more metastatic to lymphatics Understanding FGFR biology is foundational.
